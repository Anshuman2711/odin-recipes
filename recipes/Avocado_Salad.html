Description

This fresh avocado salad is a simple yet flavorful dish packed with creamy avocado, juicy tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, and a zesty lime dressing. Itâ€™s perfect as a light meal or a side dish.

The combination of fresh ingredients makes this salad refreshing and nutritious, ideal for warm weather or a healthy addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced avocados, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lime juice, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss to coat all ingredients evenly.
  4. Sprinkle fresh cilantro on top and let the salad sit for a few minutes to absorb the flavors.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
