Fresh Tomato Soup Recipe

Home

Description

This fresh tomato soup is a comforting and healthy dish, made from ripe tomatoes, aromatic herbs, and a touch of cream. It’s the perfect warm meal for chilly days.

With its rich, velvety texture and deep flavors, this homemade soup is far superior to store-bought varieties. Pair it with crispy grilled cheese or crusty bread for a classic combination.

Ingredients

  • 6 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil (or 1 tbsp fresh basil)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and garlic, sautéing until softened.
  2. Add the chopped tomatoes, salt, pepper, and basil. Cook for about 10 minutes until the tomatoes break down.
  3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for another 15 minutes.
  4. Blend the soup using an immersion blender or transfer it to a regular blender and blend until smooth.
  5. Return the soup to the pot, add butter and heavy cream (if using), and stir well.
  6. Simmer for 5 more minutes, then serve hot with fresh basil or croutons.
